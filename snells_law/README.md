## ğŸ”¬ The Physics Behind It

Snell's Law describes the relationship between the angles of incidence and refraction:

$$n_1 \sin(\theta_1) = n_2 \sin(\theta_2)$$

This simulator handles:
- **Angle Calculation:** Using `numpy.arcsin` to find the output angle.
- **Total Internal Reflection:** Logic to detect when light cannot pass through the interface.
- **Vector Visualization:** 2D plotting of incident and refracted rays.
